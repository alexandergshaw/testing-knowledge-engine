    Generate course materials

    Built your project with Copilot? Upload the repository zip and get a PPTX lecture plus Word-formatted LMS introductions and assignment instructions for every week — and a deterministic rubric.csv for automated, non-AI grading.

    Paste your module's learning objectives in any format — a list, or prose like "students will be able to…". Get a PowerPoint that explains each objective with worked examples and speaker-note talking points, built from cited sources with no AI.
